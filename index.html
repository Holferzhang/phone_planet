Various Phone Cases and Accessories


In Phone Planet, you can suitable and durable phone case and tablet case. We sell phone cover and screen protector for Apple, SamSung, Sony, HTC,Google, Huawei, Oppo, Nokia, ZTE. We only only have attractive phone case,but we also have lifeproof and other heavy duty case to protect your phone. Besides, car holder, charging cable, power band and other accessories are in Phone Planet. We make sure our customers receive high quality products and best customer service.


Professional Phone/tablet Repair

Phone Planet promise most repairs are carried out on site; All technicians have receive professional training. Most repairs will be completed within 45 minutes. Our workshop skill-set and equipment make sure that we can solve common issues like cracked screens, charging port damage, sluggish batteries, water damage or other complex issues. We fix everything from iPhones, Samsung, HTC, Sony phones and much more. 

 

We pride ourselves on our work, use the high quality parts with a three month warranty on parts and labour. You don’t need an appointment to visit Phone Planet.
